
### Problem ###
Given a series of dates in YYYY-MM-DD format, find the earliest date.

### Test Case ###
Input:
5
2023-06-14 2021-04-30 2022-09-12 2020-01-01 2021-12-25
Output:
2020-01-01

### Test Case ###
Input:
3
2019-05-20 2018-11-15 2020-07-07
